Privacy Policy

This section explains what data we collect through the website, why we collect it, which third-party services are involved, and how we handle that data.

Who we are

Lumen and Pixel is a live event production studio based in Portugal. This website is used to present our services, collect project enquiries, and allow visitors to subscribe to our newsletter.

What we collect

  • Contact form submissions, including name, company or organization, email address, selected service, and project message.
  • Newsletter signups submitted through the embedded MailerLite form.
  • Basic technical information required for website delivery through GitHub Pages and the external tools listed below.

Why we use it

  • To respond to project enquiries and other messages sent through the website.
  • To send newsletter updates to users who explicitly subscribe.
  • To deliver resources or redirect visitors to external purchase pages where relevant.

Legal bases

  • Project enquiries are processed on the basis of legitimate interest, so we can respond to incoming requests.
  • Newsletter subscriptions are processed on the basis of consent.
  • Where purchases happen through Gumroad, payment and checkout data are handled on Gumroad’s side under their own terms and privacy practices.

Third-party services used on this website

  • Web3Forms — used to receive and forward contact form submissions.
  • MailerLite — used for newsletter signup and email marketing automation.
  • Gumroad — used for external product pages and transactions.
  • GitHub Pages — used to host the website.

How long we keep data

We keep enquiry and newsletter data only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for communication, operational follow-up, or legal and administrative purposes.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, or object to certain types of processing, as applicable under relevant law. Newsletter emails also include an unsubscribe option.

How to contact us

For privacy-related requests, use the contact form on the About page.
